Kick Me

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 21, 2025

Thank you for choosing "Kick Me" to help you track your baby's kicks. Your privacy and trust are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. The short version is simple: we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All information you enter into the app stays on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

"Kick Me" is designed to be a private and secure tool for you. We want to be crystal clear: our app does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your location data.
  • Your device's unique identifiers.
  • Any data from other apps on your device.

We have no servers to store your data on, and we do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that would track you.

2. Information You Provide (Stored Locally)

Any data you enter into "Kick Me" is saved exclusively on your device's local storage. This includes:

  • The date and time of kick counting sessions.
  • The duration of each session.
  • The number of kicks recorded in each session.
  • Any notes or preferences you set within the app.

This information is for your eyes only. It allows you to view your kick counting history and use the app's features as intended. Because this data is stored locally, we (the developers) cannot access, view, or recover it for you. If you delete the app or clear the app's data from your device settings, this information will be permanently erased.

3. App Permissions

"Kick Me" does not require any special permissions to function. It does not need access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location. The app only uses your device's internal storage to save your session data locally.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used this app and entered information, you can delete that information by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it from the device.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please feel free to contact us.
